FAQ

Does threading hurt?

Most clients feel light tingling. We use skin tensioning and aloe gel to reduce sensitivity.

How long does it last?

Typically 3–4 weeks depending on hair growth. Tints last ~2 weeks on hair and a few days on skin.

Do you accept walk‑ins?

Yes. To avoid waiting, call ahead or book a time.
